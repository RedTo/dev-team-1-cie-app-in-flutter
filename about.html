Our Goal

The CiE, "Courses in English" is an application which provide detail of the courses and enrollment process to MUAS students and exchange students. The application must be simple and efficient to use for any users.

The Problem

There are several problems of the traditional way which make the users feel uncomfortable with the selection of the courses in English. The problem will be list below:
  • Based on outsource, Primuss
  • Inefficient & time consuming
  • Long non searchable list of course
  • Scattered information
  • Overlapping schedule

Our Solution

The CiE application will provide faster and simpler for the users. Some solutions are providing in the concept section. We are going to provide tools and resources for users in the application. We are going to listen to users and improve the application to guarantee that our users will come back to use our product again.
